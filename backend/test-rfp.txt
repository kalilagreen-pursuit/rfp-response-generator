REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL (RFP)
City of Springfield - IT Infrastructure Modernization Project

RFP Number: SPR-2024-IT-001
Issue Date: November 15, 2024
Due Date: December 15, 2024, 5:00 PM EST

1. INTRODUCTION AND PURPOSE

The City of Springfield is seeking qualified vendors to provide comprehensive IT infrastructure modernization services. This project aims to upgrade our aging network infrastructure, implement cloud-based solutions, and enhance cybersecurity measures across all city departments.

2. SCOPE OF WORK

The selected vendor shall provide:
- Network infrastructure assessment and upgrade
- Cloud migration services for city applications
- Implementation of enhanced cybersecurity measures
- Staff training and documentation
- 24/7 technical support for 12 months post-implementation

3. BUDGET

The estimated budget for this project is $500,000 - $750,000. Proposals exceeding this range will not be considered.

4. SUBMISSION REQUIREMENTS

All proposals must include:
- Company background and qualifications
- Technical approach and methodology
- Project timeline and milestones
- Detailed pricing breakdown
- References from similar government projects
- Proof of required certifications

5. EVALUATION CRITERIA

Proposals will be evaluated based on:
- Technical capability (30%)
- Experience and qualifications (25%)
- Price (25%)
- Project approach (20%)

6. TIMELINE

- RFP Issue Date: November 15, 2024
- Questions Due: November 25, 2024
- Responses to Questions: November 30, 2024
- Proposal Due Date: December 15, 2024, 5:00 PM EST
- Vendor Presentations: January 10-15, 2025
- Contract Award: January 31, 2025
- Project Start: February 15, 2025
